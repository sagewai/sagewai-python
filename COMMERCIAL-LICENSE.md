# Sagewai Commercial Licensing Options

## Overview

Sagewai is dual-licensed:

1. **AGPL-3.0** (free, open-source) — for individuals, companies, and organizations that comply with the AGPL-3.0 copyleft requirements
2. **Commercial License** (paid) — for organizations that need an alternative to AGPL-3.0

This client library is published under the same dual-license terms as the Sagewai platform.

## When You Need a Commercial License

You need a commercial license if you want to do ANY of the following:

- **Embed Sagewai in proprietary software** without disclosing your source code under AGPL-3.0
- **Offer Sagewai as a managed service (SaaS)** without AGPL-3.0 obligations on your platform code
- **Remove Sagewai branding** (white-label) when offering it as part of your product or service
- **Modify Sagewai** and keep modifications private (not contributing back under AGPL-3.0)

## When You Do NOT Need a Commercial License

You do **NOT** need a commercial license if:

- You use Sagewai internally within your organization (self-hosted, no SaaS offering to third parties)
- You use Sagewai and comply with AGPL-3.0 (share your modifications under AGPL-3.0)
- You contribute to Sagewai as an open-source contributor
- You use Sagewai for personal, educational, or research purposes
- You build applications using Sagewai as infrastructure (your agents and workflows are yours, not derivative works)

## Important Clarification

**Building agents with Sagewai does not make your agents derivative works.** Your agent code, workflows, prompts, and business logic are yours. The commercial license is only needed when you modify the Sagewai platform itself or offer it as a service.
